Enemies of Plants and Trees

Trees and plants have enemies. Some insects are the enemies of plants. They lay their eggs on the leaves of trees. Some animals are plant enemies, too. They eat the young leaves of plants. Rats eat the seeds of plants. They eat the young leaves of plants. Rats eat the seeds of plants. They like rice and corn. Snails are also harmful. The giant snail is the most harmful. Trees and plants are our friends. We should take care of them.
